To improve accuracy of oscillometric BP measurement:

  • Infant is calm and not moving.
  • Extremity is straight and at the level of the heart.
  • Cuff arrow overlies the artery beneath.
  • Cuff size is correct.
 
Too large a cuff will underestimate the blood pressure.
Too small a cuff will overestimate the blood pressure.
