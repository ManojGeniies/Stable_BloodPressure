Septic Shock
  • Treatment of septic shock involves a combination of the therapies used to treat hypovolemic and cardiogenic shock.
  • May require more fluid boluses because of capillary injury and leaking of fluid from the blood vessels into the interstitial space.
  • Dopamine infusion may be necessary.
  • Optimize oxygenation and ventilation.
