Neurologic Status
 
  • Poor O2 delivery to the brain and accumulation of lactic acid will cause neurologic depression.
  • Neurologic signs include:
    • Irritability initially
    • Decreased level of consciousness
    • Hypotonia
    • Lethargy
In advanced states of shock, brain injury may lead to seizures.
