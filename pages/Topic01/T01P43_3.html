Treatment of cardiogenic shock is aimed at correcting the underlying problems that negatively affect heart function.
The underlying problems include:
  • Hypoxia
  • Acidosis
  • Hypotension
  • Arrhythmias
  • Infection
  • Electrolyte and/or mineral imbalances
  • Hypoglycemia
  • Hypothermia
