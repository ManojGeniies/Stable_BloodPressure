Useful Laboratory Tests

  • Blood gas and blood lactate to assess the degree of shock
  • CBC and blood culture
    • To investigate infection, anemia, and thrombocytopenia
  • Other tests:
    • Glucose
    • Liver function tests
    • Electrolytes
    • Ionized calcium
    • Renal function tests
    • Cardiac enzymes
  • Metabolic testing if concerned about inborn errors of metabolism
