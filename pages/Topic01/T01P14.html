Assessment of pulse pressure can reveal important diagnostic clues.

  • To calculate, subtract the diastolic number from the systolic number.
  • Normal pulse pressure
    • For term infant: 25 to 30 mmHg
    • For preterm infant: 15 to 25 mmHg
Example:
Systolic – Diastolic
51 – 27 = 24
Pulse pressure = 24 mmHg
