Indications
Treat poor cardiac contractility and hypotension
Route
IV continuous infusion (IV pump)
Dose
Usual starting dose; 5 to 10 mcg/kg/minute
Range: 5 to 20 mcg/kg/minute
Dopamine administration and mixing
Rules for dopamine infusion

Precautions

  • Never give dopamine in an umbilical artery catheter or any other arterial site.
  • Whenever possible, infuse dopamine a through a central line.
  • Monitor the blood pressure and heart rate closely.
